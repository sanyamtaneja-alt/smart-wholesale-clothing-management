# Smart Wholesale Clothing Management System

## Problem Statement
Wholesale clothing merchants currently manage stock, customer orders, and payments manually using notebooks and WhatsApp. This often leads to stock mismatches, delayed order processing, duplicate entries, and missed sales opportunities.

## Proposed Solution
A simple digital management system that helps wholesalers track inventory, retailer orders, and payment dues in one place.

## Key Features
- Stock Management
- Order Tracking
- Customer Management
- Payment Due Tracking
- Business Dashboard

## Merchant Details
- Industry: Wholesale Clothing
- Location: Saharanpur
- Business Type: B2B + B2C Wholesaler

## POC Scope
The prototype demonstrates:
1. Digital stock tracking
2. Retailer order management
3. Payment due monitoring
4. Dashboard with business summary

## Expected Business Impact
- Reduce manual work by 50%+
- Reduce stock confusion
- Faster order processing
- Better payment tracking
- Fewer missed sales opportunities
